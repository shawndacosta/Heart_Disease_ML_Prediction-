## 📂 Data Availability

Due to their large size, the original training and test datasets are provided as compressed files in this repository:

- `train.zip`
- `test.zip`

They are also publicly available on Kaggle:  [Heart Disease Dataset](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/algozee/heart-decices)

The predictions generated by the final **XGBoost** model are also included as:

- `Heart_prediction_test.zip`
